## About me

I work at the intersection of **software engineering**, **data science**, **psychology**, and **mental health research**.

My focus is building practical tools that help researchers move from messy ideas and messy data to usable systems, clean analyses, and reproducible workflows.

I especially enjoy projects that combine:

- behavioral and cognitive science
- research data infrastructure
- online experiments
- health and mental health data
- AI-assisted research workflows
- fast prototyping and hackathon-style product building

### Behavioral experiments
